Solar Pigeon Guard Installation Services

One of the most common challenges faced by solar panel owners is bird nesting beneath rooftop solar systems.

The space beneath solar panels offers shade, shelter, and protection, making it highly attractive to pigeons. Without preventative measures, birds can quickly establish nests and create ongoing maintenance issues.

Why Pigeons Are Attracted to Solar Panels

Solar arrays provide:

  • Protection from predators
  • Shelter from weather
  • Cooler temperatures during summer
  • Safe nesting environments

Once pigeons settle beneath a solar system, they often return repeatedly and attract additional birds.

Problems Caused by Birds Under Solar Panels

Damaged Wiring

Birds can interfere with electrical wiring and system components.

Excessive Debris Accumulation

Nesting materials often collect beneath the panels.

Restricted Airflow

Debris can affect ventilation and system performance.

Roof Damage

Bird droppings can contribute to roof deterioration.

Fire Risks

Dry nesting materials near electrical equipment may create hazards.

Increased Maintenance Costs

Cleaning and repairs can become expensive without preventative protection.

What Is a Solar Pigeon Guard?

A Solar Pigeon Guard is a professionally installed barrier system that prevents birds from accessing the area beneath solar panels. The barrier allows proper airflow while effectively blocking entry points.
